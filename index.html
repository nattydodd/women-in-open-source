    > GitHub 2017 survey found that 3% of FLOSS contributors are women

    New for this year's event is a dedicated space to share experiences and discover Women in Tech and Open Source.

    It's no secret that there are a lack of women in our industry, or that women often choose to leave. At this event, let's get inspired by Women in Open Source and highlight them by adding them to a public repo for others to discover. Add names and links to the repo by creating Pull Requests that will count towards the Hacktoberfest challenge. This space is also the perfect place to ask questions, share experiences and network with peers and allies.
